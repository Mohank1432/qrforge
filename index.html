How to Create Custom, Privacy-First QR Codes

QR codes are everywhere—on restaurant menus, business cards, event posters, and digital advertisements. They bridge the gap between the physical and digital worlds. However, many online QR code generators track your data, limit your scans, or eventually break your links unless you pay a premium subscription. QRForge was built to solve these problems by offering a 100% free, private, and unlimited QR code generator that runs entirely in your web browser.

Why Use Client-Side QR Generation?

When you type a URL or sensitive text into most free QR generators, that data is sent to a remote server. The server generates the image and sends it back. This means the service provider knows exactly what links you are creating. Worse, some services use "dynamic tracking links," meaning if they go out of business (or if you stop paying them), your printed QR codes will stop working.

QRForge uses advanced client-side JavaScript to render the QR code directly on your device. The text or URL you paste never leaves your computer or smartphone. The resulting QR code contains the exact, static data you entered. This guarantees that your QR codes will never expire, never track users via middleman servers, and remain entirely private.
