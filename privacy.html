Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to QRForge ("we," "our," or "us"), available at qr.mktools.ink. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. QRForge is a client-side tool — all QR code generation happens entirely in your browser. We do not:

  • Store your URLs, text, or any content you enter
  • Require account creation or login
  • Track individual users
  • Send your data to any server

Local Storage: We use your browser's localStorage to save your QR code history locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared at any time using the "Clear All" button in the History section.

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to load the Inter typeface. Google may collect your IP address and browser information when fonts are loaded. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

QRForge itself does not set cookies. However, our third-party advertising partner (Google AdSense) may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of data breach from our end. All QR code generation happens locally in your browser.

6. Children's Privacy

QRForge is a general-purpose tool suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
